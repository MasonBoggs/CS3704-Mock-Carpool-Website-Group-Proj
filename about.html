Our Story

Hokie Rides was created by a group of Virginia Tech students who recognized the need for a safe, affordable, and eco-friendly transportation solution for the campus community.

With limited parking on campus and rising costs of transportation, we wanted to create a platform where students could easily connect to share rides, save money, and reduce their environmental impact.

Our Values

Safety First

We prioritize the safety of our users through verified student accounts, driver ratings, and a trusted community where you travel only with fellow Hokies.

Affordability

We believe transportation shouldn't break the bank. Our platform helps students save money by sharing the costs of travel.

Sustainability

By encouraging carpooling, we're helping reduce the carbon footprint of our campus community and working toward a greener future.

Community

Beyond just transportation, we're building connections between students who might not otherwise meet, strengthening our Hokie community.

Our Mission

At Hokie Rides, our mission is to revolutionize campus transportation by creating a safe, affordable, and sustainable carpooling platform exclusively for the Virginia Tech community.

We aim to reduce traffic congestion around campus, decrease the demand for limited parking spaces, lower transportation costs for students, and reduce our collective carbon footprint.
